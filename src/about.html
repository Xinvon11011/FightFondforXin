About RealXin AI Floorplan Generator

The RealXin AI Floorplan Generator is a cutting-edge tool designed to help users visualize and generate detailed floorplans of houses based on uploaded images and descriptions. This tool leverages machine learning and artificial intelligence to provide accurate and fast results.

Key Features

  • Upload up to 40 images to generate corresponding house floorplans.
  • Utilizes TensorFlow.js and ML5.js for image processing and floorplan creation.
  • Interactive canvas with zoom-in/zoom-out capabilities for floorplan viewing.
  • Download the generated floorplans in high-quality PNG format.
  • Real-time image previews and modification capabilities.

How It Works

Once you upload your house images, our AI processes each image to detect architectural features and generate floorplans. You can then preview, modify, and download the generated floorplans for your use.

Why Use RealXin?

RealXin provides a unique combination of advanced AI-driven tools with easy-to-use interfaces, making it ideal for architects, designers, and homeowners who want to visualize their dream houses or explore various architectural designs.
